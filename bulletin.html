NASP BULLETIN

Welcome to the Bulletin Archives of the Nepal Association for the Study of Pain (NASP). Here, you'll discover a collection of insightful publications covering various facets of pain management. Our bulletins feature original research articles, case reports, meta-analyses, review articles, and letters to the editor. As you navigate through these pages, you'll gain valuable insights into the activities of NASP and stay abreast of the latest developments in the field of pain studies worldwide. We invite you to explore this repository of knowledge and contribute to the ongoing discourse on effective pain management.
