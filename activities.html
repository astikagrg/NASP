Relief: NASP Activities

Welcome to the Nepal Association for the Study of Pain (NASP) Activities Page. We organize Continuing Medical Education (CME) programs, interactive workshops, and community awareness events to advance knowledge in pain management. Stay updated on our initiatives to enhance pain care and education in Nepal.

How we are advancing pain management and education

Continuing Medical Education (CME)

Offering healthcare professionals opportunities to enhance their expertise. Keeping professionals updated on the latest advancements in pain studies

Updates and Events

Stay tuned for the latest information on our diverse range of activities. Contributing to the overall improvement of pain care and education in Nepal.

Interactive Workshops

Providing hands-on learning experiences. Facilitating skill development in pain management techniques.

Community Awareness Programs

Disseminating valuable information to the public. Promoting a better understanding of pain and its management.
