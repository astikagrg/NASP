KNOW ABOUT US

Transforming Pain
Management in Nepal

Pain is a natural alert system, but chronic pain can disrupt lives and increase healthcare costs. The Nepal Association for the Study of Pain (NASP), established in 2016, is dedicated to improving pain management in Nepal. Founded by Prof. Balkrishna Bhattarai and Dr. Asish Subedi, NASP aims to guide and advocate for effective pain relief, enhancing the well-being of countless Nepalese.
